Smt. Swati Prabhakar Raykar - Founder

Founders Message

Every sacred journey begins with faith, and every destination reveals itself only to those who travel with devotion in their hearts. By the grace of God, I have been fortunate to experience many joyful and diverse journeys throughout my life. My travel journey began in the 7th grade with a memorable boat trip to Goa. Before I even turned 17, I had already explored adventure sports, starting with a one-month mountaineering training program and eventually experiencing activities such as skydiving. Along the way, I also explored destinations like the Andaman Islands, went on jungle safaris, and traveled to many places both within India and abroad. My spiritual travel journey began with the Char Dham Yatra, and over time it expanded to include pilgrimages such as Amarnath, Vaishno Devi, Kardali Van Parikrama, Girnar Parikrama, Swargarohini, Panch Prayag, the Narmada Parikrama on foot along the riverbanks, and the Braj Chaurasi Kos Parikrama, among many others. From jungle safaris to international travel and profound spiritual pilgrimages, my journey has been truly diverse. The list of places is very long, but I have mentioned some of the important journeys here. With the intention of sharing the benefit of these travel experiences, along with 25 years of experience with LaxmiNarayan Tours & Travels, I started Radharamanji Yatra Parivaar so that more people can undertake spiritual journeys at an affordable cost. Radhe Radhe 🙏
